About Us

Alexander Mendez

Alexander Mendez is an Astronomer and Data Scientist. After graduating with his PhD in Astrophysics from the University of California San Diego, Alex decided to pursue his passion and continue his research of the universe as a post-doctorate at the Johns Hopkins University and moved to Baltimore in August of 2014. One of his most recent and greatest accomplishments has been finding his soulmate.

Kathleen Mason

Kathleen Mason is a public health researcher and data scientist. She is currently pursuing a Masters in Government Analytics at The Johns Hopkins University with a focus on data science for public policy. She feels blessed to have finally found her half-orange.

How we met

Alex and Katie first met in September 2014 at Felici’s Cafe for coffee and smoothies in Baltimore, Maryland. They were pleasantly delighted to meet each other’s acquaintance. On their second date, Katie and Alex went on their first bicycle ride. They took the Baltimore-Annapolis rail trail, stopping by Sofi’s crepes along the way. They were both smitten.
It was love at first bike.

Katie and Alex have had many adventures since they first met. They have hiked the Great Falls, driven along the skyline drive in Shenandoah, hung out with hipsters in Brooklyn in New York, and enjoyed beautiful waterfalls in both the North Carolina Stone Mountain State Park and the Pennsylvania Ohiophile State Park.

Most important of all, they feel blessed to have had the privilege to share these memories with their friends and family.
